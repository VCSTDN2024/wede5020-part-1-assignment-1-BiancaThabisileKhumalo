1. Name of the Website
Urban Auto Hub

2. History of the Business
Urban Auto Hub was founded in 2023 in Johannesburg, South Africa, as a modern alternative to traditional car service centers. It was established by a team of young automotive professionals and entrepreneurs who identified a gap in the market for tech-forward, urban-friendly vehicle services.
The company started by offering express car maintenance in business parks and city centers, later expanding into electric vehicle (EV) services, vehicle infotainment upgrades, and premium car detailing.
The idea was simple: bring convenience, speed, and smart technology to South African vehicle owners who live busy urban lives.
Urban Auto Hub operates with a focus on efficiency, digital integration, and eco-conscious service options, setting itself apart as a forward-thinking brand in the automotive service industry.

3. Target Audience
Urban Auto Hub is designed for modern, tech-savvy car owners who value convenience, speed, and innovation. Key target segments include:
•	Urban Professionals and Commuters: Individuals who live and work in cities, often with busy schedules and limited time for car maintenance.
•	Young Entrepreneurs and Fleet Owners: Small business owners or startups needing quick, reliable vehicle services.
•	EV Owners and Early Adopters: Drivers interested in or already using electric vehicles and looking for expert support and education.
•	Car Enthusiasts: Customers interested in tech upgrades, infotainment, and premium detailing services.
•	Environmentally Conscious Drivers: People prioritizing sustainability, eco-friendly products, and modern automotive trends.
The website and services will cater to customers primarily in Johannesburg and other metropolitan areas across South Africa.

4. Goals and Objectives
4. Goals and Objectives
Primary Goals
•	Deliver fast, reliable car services tailored to modern urban lifestyles.
•	Establish a strong online presence with digital booking and service transparency.
•	Promote sustainable vehicle care with support for EVs and eco-friendly products.
•	Build a community of engaged car owners through content, loyalty rewards, and education.
Specific Objectives
•	Launch a mobile-friendly, modern website that clearly presents Urban Auto Hub’s brand and offerings.
•	Enable online booking and real-time service availability.
•	Educate users through blogs, videos, and EV-readiness checklists.
•	Integrate social media and WhatsApp support into the customer journey.
•	Increase online bookings by at least 30% within the first six months by using a simple, user-friendly booking system and targeted promotions.

5. Website Functionality
Core Features
•	Home Page: Highlighted services, customer benefits, special offers, and latest blog/video.
•	About Us: Company history, team background, mission, and values (innovation, speed, sustainability).
•	Services: Organized into categories with visuals and pricing:
o	Express Maintenance (oil change, brake checks, battery)
o	Car Detailing & Wrapping
o	Infotainment & Tech Upgrades (touchscreens, reverse cameras)
o	Electric Vehicle Support (charging advice, system checks)
•	Online Booking Form: Choose a service, location, date, and time. Confirmation via email or WhatsApp.
•	EV Education Page: Guides on owning and maintaining electric vehicles in South Africa.
•	Blog/Vlog Section: Articles and short videos on car care, EV tips, smart driving habits.
•	Customer Reviews: Testimonials from city-based clients, small fleet managers, and first-time EV owners.
•	Contact Page: Google Maps integration, phone, WhatsApp button, and contact form.
•	Live Chat & WhatsApp Integration: Quick answers, appointment confirmations, and follow-up reminders.
•	Social Media Links: Active profiles on TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook with car hacks and service demos.
Optional Add-ons
•	Loyalty Program Portal: Track visits and rewards for frequent customers.
•	EV Battery Health Tracker: Submit info and get personalized feedback.
•	Mobile App (Future Phase): For instant bookings, reminders, and updates.

6. Design and user experience
Colour Scheme
Urban Auto Hub’s palette will reflect professionalism, tech-forward thinking, and urban sophistication:
Element	Colour	Purpose
Primary Colour	Deep Blue (#003366)	Trust, technology, and reliability
Secondary Colour	Slate Grey (#666666)	Neutral balance, urban tone
Accent Colour	Light Blue (#3399FF)	Highlights, buttons, links
Background	White (#FFFFFF)	Clean, minimal, easy readability
Call-to-Action (CTA)	Electric Blue (#00BFFF)	Draw attention to booking and promotions
Typography
Simple, modern fonts that support readability and professionalism:
•	Headings: Montserrat Bold – clean, geometric, ideal for titles
•	Body Text: Open Sans Regular – highly legible, neutral tone
•	Buttons/Labels: Roboto Medium – compact and functional
Logo Placement
Top-right corner, consistent across all pages. Logo should be minimal, possibly a stylized car silhouette or hub icon in blue and grey.
Layout and Design Principles
•	Centered, Balanced Layouts: Content blocks will be evenly spaced with generous padding.
•	Visual Hierarchy: Clear separation between headings, subheadings, and body text.
•	Mobile-First Design: Responsive layout with collapsible menus and thumb-friendly buttons.
•	Whitespace Usage: Ample spacing to avoid clutter and guide user focus.
•	Navigation Simplicity: Sticky top nav bar with icons and dropdowns for Services, Booking, Blog, Contact.
 User Experience Considerations
•	Fast Load Times: Optimized images and lightweight code.
•	Accessibility: High contrast, alt text for images, keyboard navigation.
•	Booking Flow: 3-click process — select service → choose date/time → confirm via WhatsApp/email.
•	Trust Signals: Testimonials, Google Maps integration, SSL badge, and social proof.
•	Content Strategy: Blog and EV Education pages will use short paragraphs, bullet points, and embedded videos for quick consumption.

Common Page structure:

1. index.html (Home Page)
Function:
•	This is the landing page and first impression for visitors.
•	Highlights key services, promotions, and benefits of Urban Auto Hub.
•	Includes calls-to-action like “Book Now” or links to the EV Education and Services pages.
•	May also showcase featured blog posts or videos to engage visitors.

2. about.html (About Us Page)
Function:
•	Provides the history of Urban Auto Hub, mission, vision, and values.
•	Introduces the team and founders.
•	Builds trust and credibility with users by sharing the story behind the company.
•	Explains the company’s focus on modern urban vehicle services and EV support.

3. services.html (Services Page)
Function:
•	Displays a detailed list of all services Urban Auto Hub offers, grouped into categories:
o	Express Maintenance
o	Car Detailing & Wrapping
o	Infotainment & Tech Upgrades
o	Electric Vehicle Support
•	May include pricing, visuals, or short descriptions.
•	Allows users to decide which service to book and understand what the company offers.

4. booking.html (Online Booking Page)
Function:
•	Enables users to schedule appointments online.
•	Includes a form to select service, location, date, and time.
•	Can send confirmation emails or WhatsApp messages.
•	Helps increase bookings and streamline service management for the business.

5. contact.html (Contact Page)
Function:
•	Allows users to reach out directly to Urban Auto Hub via a contact form.
•	Provides alternative contact methods: phone, WhatsApp, location, and Google Maps.
•	The live map helps users locate the business easily.
•	Serves as a hub for inquiries, complaints, or feedback.

6. ev-education.html (EV Education Page)
Function:
•	Educates visitors about electric vehicle ownership in South Africa.
•	Provides guides, checklists, and tips for EV maintenance.
•	Can include videos, blog posts, and infographics.
•	Positions Urban Auto Hub as an expert in modern and sustainable automotive solutions.

7. blog.html (Blog/Vlog Page)
Function:
•	Contains articles and short videos about car care, EV tips, smart driving habits, and automotive trends.
•	Drives engagement, repeat visits, and SEO traffic.
•	Builds authority in the automotive services space.

8. reviews.html (Customer Reviews Page)
Function:
•	Displays testimonials and feedback from previous clients.
•	Helps build trust with potential customers.
•	Can include ratings, photos of serviced vehicles, and fleet client feedback.

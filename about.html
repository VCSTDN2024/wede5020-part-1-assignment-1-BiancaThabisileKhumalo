History of the Business

Founded in 2023 in Johannesburg by young automotive professionals, Urban Auto Hub offers tech-forward, urban-friendly vehicle services.

We started with express maintenance in city centers and expanded into EV support, infotainment upgrades, and premium detailing.

Our mission is to make car care convenient, affordable, and eco-conscious for urban drivers.
